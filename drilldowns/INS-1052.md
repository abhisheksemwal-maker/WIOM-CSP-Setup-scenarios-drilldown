## Respectful plural rule
`काम कर रहे हैं` — NOT `काम कर रहा है`. This rule applies anywhere the CSP is addressing or referring to an executor. Applies to:
- INS-1044 (self, Annu)
- INS-1052 (delegated, Sunil)
- INS-1045 (delegated, Rajesh — even in past-tense `देरी कर दी`)
